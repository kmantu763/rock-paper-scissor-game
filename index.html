About Game :-

Rock, paper, scissors is a game played between two players. Here ONE Player is "YOU" and another Player is your "Computer". The goal is to beat the computer by choosing correct things, rock, paper, or scissors.

How to Play :-

  1. First you choose anything Rock, Paper or Scissor.
  2. Computer will also choose Rock, Paper or Scissor randomly.
  3. Rock blunts (beats) Scissor, Scissor cut (beats) Paper, and Parer wraps (beats) Rock.
  4. If both choose same thing , no winner, game will Draw.
